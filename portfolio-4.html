MOHP

UI/UX Development

This project really helped me to expand my knowledge of Web Development and UI/UX Development. I created for a company called Mobile Oral Health Professionals, or MOHP for short. In all, I designed their logo, website (www.mobileoralhealth.com) and the UI/UX for a future app. The logo was created with Photoshop cs6 and Sketch, and attempts to relay to customers the purpose of the company (that it provides mobile dental cleanings) in a simple yet effective way (it's a tooth with a car incorporated into it!). The company's website was a great way to expand my knowledge in the area of web development, and was built with bootstrap. Finally, the UI/UX for the MOHP application really helped to solidify my understanding of basic design principles such as use of whitespace and colourscheme. It was a job that seemed more like a personal project as a result of all the learning I did!
