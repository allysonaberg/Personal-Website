ScaryBot

A Facebook Messenger Chatbot

ScaryBot is a Facebook Messenger chatbot that provides you with a curated list of the best Creepypastas on the internet. Throughout this project, I worked with technologies such as:

  • Node.js
  • Natural Language Processing/Understanding, via Wit.ai
  • Heroku (hosting and deployemnt)
  • Database CRUD operations, using MongoDB and Mongoose
  • The Facebook Messenger SDK

Overall, the launch of the project was a success! ScaryBot gathered over 180 users within its first three days of release, and was featured on BotList! After its initial release, I was given the chance to analyze scaryBot's interactions with users to see the areas most in need of improvement. The most common issues users ran into was with requesting commands that the bot could not recognize. This lead me to move towards Natural Language Processing and Understanding: A form of AI which would allow the bot to "recognize" what the user is asking without having each scenario hard-coded.
