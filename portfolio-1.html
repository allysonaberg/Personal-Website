HydraChew

An entrepreneural project

HydraChew was an entrepreneural challenge I took part in as part of the SHAD program. We were challenged to innovate a solution to the problem: "Why don't you play outside?". Our product was HydraChew, a gum that reduces risk of dehydration by alerting the user of their hydration level using the pH of their saliva. A large part of my contribution to this project was the design of the marketing materials and logos. Overall, the experience taught me not only about the hardships of entrepreneurship, but about elements of design and how to design something specifically for the customer rather than the company.
